Our God Reigns Model School Cultural

Bushenyi Parents' School is a private mixed primary institution dedicated to providing academic excellence within a strong Christian foundation.
We are conveniently located on the Bushenyi-Mbarara road in Bushenyi town, Western Uganda.
We offer comprehensive Day and Full-Boarding programs, ensuring a secure and disciplined environment where children can grow both academically and spiritually.
Our education rigorously follows the Uganda National Standard Curriculum, enriched by principles of faith.

Academics

At Bushenyi Parents' School, academic excellence is our non-negotiable standard. We rigorously deliver the full Uganda National Standard Primary Curriculum, ensuring a deep and comprehensive foundation for every pupil.

Morals

While we relentlessly pursue academic excellence and encourage sporting success, every lesson and activity is guided by our commitment to character development. We instill core values—rooted in our faith-based foundation—including respect, honesty, responsibility, integrity, and discipline.

Sports

At Bushenyi Parents' School, we believe that a well-rounded education requires a balance between academics, spiritual growth, and physical development. Our comprehensive sports program is designed to nurture health, talent, and vital life skills.
